VibeActive
Find the perfect activities to do to make you feel better based on your current mood.
VibeActive is a mood-based fitness recommendation platform that helps users stay active by suggesting personalized physical activities tailored to their current emotional state. Using a simple checkbox interface, users indicate how they feel (e.g., happy, sad, tired, energy, stressed, angry), and the app recommends activities based on their mood. By combining mental and physical health, VibeActive makes fitness fun, accessible, and emotionally rewarding. Whether you're feeling energetic and want to dance or stressed and need to unwind with yoga, VibeActive has the perfect activity to match your vibe.
